## Description

Stylomech extracts stylometric features from text pairs to verify authorship in English and Romanized Sinhala, enabling accurate author comparison with limited data.

## Motivation

This paper demonstrates a hybrid approach that can be extended with transformer embeddings for robust authorship verification in low-resource or multilingual settings.
