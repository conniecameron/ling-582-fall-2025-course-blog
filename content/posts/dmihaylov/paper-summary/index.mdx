## Description

_In your own words, what is this paper about?_
<p>The paper I have chosen utilized BERTopic, a modern NLP topic-modeling method, to 42,422 traumatic brain injury (TBI) research articles published starting from 1990. The authors extracted 27 data-driven research themes and used linear regression trend analyses to statistically distinguish "hot" and "cold" research topics in TBI. The paper demonstrates how NLP and statistical modeling can uncover shifts in scientific focus across decades of literature with less of a reliance on human review.</p>
