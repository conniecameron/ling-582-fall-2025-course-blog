This page lists the competition winners and describes the criteria for winning each category.

# Criteria

- The participant with the best summary will receive 1 bonus point on their overall course grade

- The participant with the **most creative approach** will receive 1 bonus point on their overall course grade

- The participant with the **most interpretable system** will receive 1 bonus point on their overall course grade

- The participant with the **most easily reproducible system** will receive 1 bonus point on their overall course grade

- The participant with the **best-performing system** will receive 0.5 bonus points on their overall course grade
